Utah, United States of America

Latter-day saints settled Utah back in 1847. Initially, it wasn't a very appealing place, but with hard work and the help of God, the saints transformed it into a beautiful, productive land. Today, Utah holds a reputation as one of the most family-friendly states. It has many different industries and even hosted the Winter Olympics in 2002. Utah apparently made a good impression, as it will host the Winter Games in 2034 as well!
